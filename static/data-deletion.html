Data Deletion Instructions

How to Request Your Data to Be Deleted

If you have used Facebook Login in our application, you can request the deletion of your personal data associated with your Facebook account in one of the following ways:

Direct Request via Email

Send an email to:

info@taptap.name

with the subject line:
“Facebook Data Deletion Request”

Please include:

  • Your Facebook User ID, or
  • The email linked to your Facebook account, or
  • Any other identifier used in our app.

We will process your request within 30 days, as required by Facebook policy.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving a valid request, we will permanently delete:

  • Facebook User ID
  • Profile name and profile picture (if stored)
  • Any app activity associated with your Facebook account
  • Any game progress linked exclusively to Facebook Login
  • All analytics or attribution data tied to your Facebook ID

This action is irreversible.

Data We May Retain

We may retain certain non-personal, aggregated, or anonymized data for:

  • security
  • fraud prevention
  • legal compliance

but nothing that can identify you as a user.

Confirmation of Deletion

When your request is completed, we will send a confirmation email.
No further action on your part is needed.
